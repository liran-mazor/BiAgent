## Executive Summary

2024 closed at **$3.77M** — 11% above our $3.4M target. Books was the standout: $668K vs $660K target, driven by 14 corporate B2B clients and 580 subscription box members by December. Electronics held steady. Sports return rate reached 9.4% by Q4 — almost at target.

2025 goal: **$4.6M revenue** (+22% YoY), EBITDA breakeven across the full year (not just H2), and shifting the category mix away from Electronics toward Books and Sports.

The central bet: Books and Sports combined reach **$1.15M** in 2025. If they don't, the fallback is a Clothing promotional push in Q3 — Clothing has reliable conversion rates and a loyal repeat-purchase base that responds to targeted email campaigns.

## Category Strategy

### Electronics — Hold and Optimize
- 2024 revenue: $1.38M (37% of total). 2025 target: $1.44M (+4%).
- No aggressive growth. Gross margin is 28% — below every other category.
- Strategy: shift mix toward audio and peripherals (42% gross margin) away from commodity devices.
- Price floor: no Electronics item below $49 listed from Q2 onward.
- Return rate target: 4.8% (down from 5.1% in 2024).

### Clothing — Steady Growth
- 2024 revenue: $812K. 2025 target: $920K (+13%).
- Return rate reached 7.3% in 2024 — best-ever. Target 6.8% in 2025.
- Best repeat-purchase rate: 2.6x annual repurchase vs 1.2x Electronics.
- Strategy: seasonal bundles in Q2/Q3, loyalty-priced reorder emails, expand size range.

### Home — Moderate Growth
- 2024 revenue: $578K. 2025 target: $660K (+14%).
- Q2 and Q3 are strong (spring cleaning, moving season). Q4 is historically weak for Home.
- Strategy: "home refresh" bundle expanded to 4-item sets at 8% discount. Expected to lift avg basket.
- Return rate target: 7.5% (down from 8.2% in 2024). Remaining issue: medium-sized furniture items.

### Books — High Growth Target
- 2024 revenue: $668K (second-highest category by revenue, highest by margin).
- 2025 target: $840K (+26%).
- Gross margin: 71%. Every $1 of Books revenue is worth more than any other category.
- B2B channel: 14 corporate clients in 2024. Target 24 clients in 2025.
- Subscription box: 580 members end-2024. Target 1,200 members by December 2025.
- Return rate target: 2.0% (down from 2.3% in 2024).

### Sports — Accelerate
- 2024 revenue: $334K. 2025 target: $740K (+121%).
- Most underpenetrated category relative to market opportunity.
- Return rate was 9.4% in Q4 2024 — first quarter below 10%. Target 8.5% for full year 2025.
- Strategy: Q1 new year fitness demand, Q2 outdoor season, Q3 back-to-school youth sports.
- Dedicated Sports merchandiser (hired February 2024) has full catalog refresh planned for Q1.
- Sports buying budget: $200K in H1, $150K in H2 (reduce seasonal overstock risk).

| Category   | 2024 Actual | 2025 Target | Primary Reason      | Secondary Reason    |

| Sports      | 9.4%       | 8.5%        | changed_mind        | wrong_size          |

**Returns cost $309K in 2024.** At 2025 revenue targets, maintaining 8.2% would cost $377K. Getting to 7.5% saves $32K. Not transformative — return reduction is now a margin story, not a survival story.

## Pricing Strategy

### No broad discounting in 2025
The board approved removing the site-wide 15% welcome discount that has been running since 2022. It attracted one-time buyers with low LTV. Replacement: 8% "starter kit" discount on first bundle purchase only.

### Price increases
- Books: 4% average increase across catalog (demand is inelastic for our B2B SKUs).
- Electronics: hold commodity prices; raise proprietary brand prices 6%.
- Sports: no change — still building category trust; price competition is higher than other categories.

### Dynamic pricing expansion
Q3: expand dynamic pricing from 30 Electronics SKUs (piloted in 2024) to full Electronics catalog. Margin improvement from pilot: 3.8% — above the 3% threshold set for rollout.
