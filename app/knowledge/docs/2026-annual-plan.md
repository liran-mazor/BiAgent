## Executive Summary

2025 closed at **$4.73M** — 3% above our $4.6M board commitment. Books exceeded plan by 8% ($905K vs $840K target), driven by B2B channel additions and subscription box growth to 1,180 members by December. Sports came in at $672K, slightly below the $680K target — H2 overstock forced end-of-season markdowns. EBITDA was breakeven for the full year, meeting the board covenant for the first time in company history.

2026 goal: **$5.5M revenue** (+16% YoY), EBITDA positive for the full year (not just breakeven), and executing the EMEA pilot in H2. The board approved three structural bets at the December 2025 meeting: UK market entry in Q3, Books subscription box scaling to 2,500 members, and Sports achieving a return rate below 8% for the full year.

Q1 is the foundations quarter. No January promotion. Sports benefits from New Year fitness demand. Books enters reading season. The Q1 target is conservative by design — setting up a clean H2 runway.

## Category Strategy

### Electronics — Optimize Margin, Stable Volume
- 2025 actual: $1.52M. 2026 target: $1.75M (+15%).
- Dynamic pricing expanded to full catalog in Q3 2025. Margin improvement: 4.1% — above the 3% threshold. Expand to Clothing accessories in Q2 2026.
- Return rate was 4.9% in 2025. Target: 4.5%.
- No new SKU categories. Audio and peripherals remain the margin focus.

### Clothing — Retention and Loyalty
- 2025 actual: $910K. 2026 target: $1.02M (+12%).
- Loyalty-priced reorder emails drove 2.9x annual repurchase. This is the lever — not new customer acquisition.
- Size guide now covers 100% of SKUs. Return rate: 7.1% in 2025. Target: 6.5%.
- Q2 seasonal bundle expansion: 6-item sets at 10% discount (previously 4-item at 8%).

### Home — Steady Execution
- 2025 actual: $672K. 2026 target: $780K (+16%).
- Medium-sized furniture re-listed in Q2 2025 with video content — return rate held at 7.9%. Continue expanding this segment.
- "Home refresh" bundle (4-item sets) performing well. Expand to 5-item premium tier in Q2.
- Return rate target: 7.2% (down from 7.9% in 2025).

### Books — Subscription and B2B Scale
- 2025 actual: $905K. 2026 target: $1.15M (+27%).
- Subscription box: 1,180 members end-2025. Target 2,500 by December 2026.
- B2B corporate clients: 22 end-2025. Target 38 clients in 2026.
- UK pilot will test Books demand in EMEA. Physical books only for EMEA in 2026.
- Return rate target: 1.9% (down from 2.1% in 2025).
- Gross margin remains 71% — every incremental Books dollar is worth more than any other category.

### Sports — Mature and Profitable
- 2025 actual: $672K. 2026 target: $800K (+19%).
- Return rate was 8.8% in 2025 — first year below 9%. Target: 7.8% in 2026.
- H2 overstock issue in 2025 was structural: bought too much summer inventory. 2026 buying budget: $180K H1 (flat), $110K H2 (reduced from $150K).
- Q1 New Year fitness demand is the biggest Sports window. Sports gets the largest share of Q1 marketing budget.
- Youth sports segment (back-to-school Q3) underperformed in 2025. Dedicated focus in 2026 with curated youth team-sport bundles.

## Q1 2026 Priorities

1. **Sports New Year push** — largest single Q1 investment ($48K of Q1 marketing budget). Fitness equipment, outdoor prep, resolution-buyer targeting. Window closes by February 15.
2. **Books reading season** — January reading lists published January 6. Target: 3 new B2B clients signed by March 31.
3. **Subscription box milestone** — reach 1,400 members by March 31 (from 1,180 at year-end).
4. **EMEA vendor contracts** — finalize UK fulfillment partner by February 28. Pilot launch date: July 1.
5. **Sports H1 buying** — commit to buying budget by March 15 before summer inventory window closes.
6. **No January promotion** — any promotional discount in January requires CFO and CEO approval. This is a standing rule.
